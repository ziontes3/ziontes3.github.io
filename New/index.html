Ethiopia's traditional clothes are usually considered to be beautiful, white woven cotton clothing seen in many parts of the country. It is made of cotton that is woven together in long strips and then sewn together to make cloth.Traditional women's clothes are called habesha qemis. These clothes are made from a cloth called shemma which are woven in long strips and then sewn together. Shiny threads are often woven into the fabric and patterns are frequently added to the garments. It takes about two to three weeks to make enough cloth for a dress.
